Aim of the game:

To find the necromancer by and defeat it before he takes complete control of the city.

********************************************

Basic actions:
- Walk
- Run
- Fight
- Change weapon
- Pick up loot
- Use items
- Rest
- Save/load
- Win/lose

Basic effects:
- lose/gain health
- Bleed
- Buff/debuff
- Death
- Probability determined attacks
- Effective/ineffective weapons
